Big Data Summer Camp

Sat 20 June 2015 | tags: BigData, -- (permalink)

Last week I gave a presentation on applied SQL at the third annual UM Big Data Summer Camp. I was happy to give back to this community given all I had learned at camp the previous summer. For my presentation / demonstration, I used data from the "Motor City Mapping" survey of property conditions in Detroit. This parcel-by-parcel survey took place late last year, and these data are publicly available via Data Driven Detroit. My slides and the prepared data are available here.
